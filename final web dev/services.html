Comprehensive Dental Solutions

IMPLANTS

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots, typically made of titanium, that are surgically placed into the jawbone to support replacement teeth or bridges. They offer a durable and long-lasting solution for missing teeth, closely mimicking natural tooth function and appearance. Implants help preserve jaw structure, prevent bone loss, and restore proper chewing ability. We use advanced 3D scanning for precise placement.

Schedule Implant Consultation

ROOT CANAL THERAPY (RCT)

A root canal is a dental procedure used to treat infection or damage to the soft pulp inside a tooth. It involves removing the infected or damaged pulp, cleaning and disinfecting the area, and then filling and sealing the tooth to prevent future infection. Our single-sitting RCT ensures minimal discomfort and preserves your natural tooth structure.

Schedule RCT

DENTAL CROWNS & BRIDGES

Dental crowns are tooth-shaped caps placed over damaged or decayed teeth to restore their shape, size, strength, and appearance. They protect weak teeth and can be used as abutments for bridges to replace missing teeth. We offer a range of materials including Zirconia, Emax, PFM, and Metal crowns.

Schedule Consultation

SCALING & PROFESSIONAL CLEANING

Scaling involves using specialized ultrasonic tools to remove hardened plaque and tartar (calculus) buildup from tooth surfaces and below the gum line. This routine procedure is essential for preventing gum disease (gingivitis/periodontitis), bad breath, and maintaining optimal oral hygiene.

Book Cleaning Session

DENTAL JEWELRY / GEMS

Tooth jewels are small, decorative adornments safely bonded to the tooth surface using a special adhesive. The process is generally quick, painless, and non-invasive, taking about 10-15 minutes. Add a sparkle to your smile!

Book Appointment

ORTHODONTIC TREATMENT (BRACES)

Orthodontic treatment focuses on correcting misaligned teeth and jaws to improve oral health, function, and aesthetics. We provide various options, including traditional metal braces and aesthetic ceramic braces, tailored to achieve a straight and beautiful smile.

Schedule Ortho Consult

CLEAR ALIGNERS

Aligner treatment, such as invisible braces, is a modern method of straightening teeth using custom-made, removable plastic aligners. They are virtually invisible, making them a discreet and comfortable alternative to fixed braces, and can be removed for eating and brushing.

Schedule Aligner Consult

COSMETIC / AESTHETIC DENTAL TREATMENT

Cosmetic dentistry enhances the look of your smile while keeping it healthy and natural. Services include Teeth Whitening to safely remove stains, Veneers to correct discoloration and gaps, and Bonding to repair small chips and cracks. Our goal is to achieve a natural-looking, radiant smile.

SMILE DESIGNING

Smile Designing is a customized treatment plan that combines different cosmetic and restorative procedures—such as whitening, veneers, bonding, and gum contouring—to improve the overall appearance of your smile. It is tailored to your facial features and personal preferences to give you a natural, confident look.

Book Consultation

GUM / GINGIVAL SURGERIES

We provide advanced treatment for periodontitis (severe gum disease) and offer cosmetic procedures like gum contouring to fix gummy smiles or reshape uneven gum lines. Our focus is on restoring the health and aesthetics of your gums, which form the foundation of your smile.

COMPLETE AND PARTIAL DENTURES

Dentures are removable appliances that can replace missing teeth and restore your smile and chewing function. We offer both complete dentures (when all teeth are missing) and partial dentures (when some natural teeth remain), customized for comfort and fit.

WISDOM TOOTH EXTRACTION

Extraction of third molars (wisdom teeth) is performed when they are impacted, causing pain, infection, or overcrowding. Our specialists ensure a comfortable and safe procedure to prevent future dental issues.

Laser technology is used for precise and minimally invasive treatments, including gum therapy, frenectomy, and minor soft tissue procedures. Laser surgery offers faster healing, reduced bleeding, and less post-operative discomfort compared to traditional methods.

TREATMENT FOR BROKEN AND UNEVEN TEETH

We provide restorative solutions for chipped, fractured, or uneven teeth using composite bonding, dental veneers, or crowns. These treatments restore the tooth's form, function, and aesthetic appeal in a seamless, natural way.
